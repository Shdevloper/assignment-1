Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

During summer, staying hydrated and cool is essential. Drink plenty of water, consume seasonal fruits like watermelon, and wear light, breathable clothing. Protect yourself from the sun with sunscreen, hats, and sunglasses.

Sustainability

Sustainability focuses on meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs.It emphasizes responsible use of resources, reducing waste, adopting renewable energy, and protecting ecosystems.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the backbone of economic and social development, encompassing transportation, energy, water supply, communication, and public facilities. It ensures connectivity, supports industries, and enhances quality of life.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of enabling individuals or groups to gain control over their lives, make informed decisions, and achieve their full potential. It involves providing access to education, resources, opportunities, and rights while fostering confidence and self-reliance.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
